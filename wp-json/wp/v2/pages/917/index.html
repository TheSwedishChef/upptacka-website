Simply Word and Spirit dissects the division between people and churches \u201cof the Word\u201d and those \u201cof the Spirit\u201d, then illuminates the path of power through unification.
\nRuth Lorensson crosses cultural chasms to show the consequences of this divide, and what it\u2019s causing us to miss out on. After debunking division, Ruth uses scripture & personal stories to show that the Word and Spirit must be held together in order to access the cumulative power that was always by-design.<\/p><\/blockquote>\n

However, between the calling and our release into the public domain comes The Wilderness!
